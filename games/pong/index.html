Click the flag to start the game. use your mouse to move the paddle around the sprite. Every time the ball hits the paddle, you get a point. And if the ball hits the bottom red line, the game ends and you have to start it all over again. Good luck!

***Please note that this is a Flash game, meaning it may not work on all devices.
