About this Site

I have always had a fascination of the HTML sites made in the late 90s and early 2000s. They always had an appealing look to me with the blend of different colors and all the cool effects that were on them, and I also couldn't forget about the classic buttons! Because of that, I wanted to make my own site with that classic theme. The problem was, I had no idea how to make a complete site like this from scratch. So after seeing a site themed to this that looked amazing, I tried to make one like it. Instead of copying the whole site directly, all I used from it was the theme. That means that all the content I put on here is of my work, but I give credit to the theme from the original author who has credit at the bottom of every page I make. Thank you for taking the time to visit, I hope you enjoy this site!
